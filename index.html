I am a doctoral student of Organizational Theory in Business Administration at the Gies College of Business, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I am currently in my second year of graduate studies.

My research interests and active projects are three-fold: (a) organizational culture and it's influence on institutions (b) Innovation Ecosystems and (c) Digital Governance of AI and Big Data

My working papers include an empirical paper on national innovation ecosystem in G20 countries and a theoretical paper on digital governance. ons, I examine how personnel expertise and capabilities affect organizational performance, such as patenting innovations. Ongoing work ties these two streams of research together, looking at how individuals' organizational fit interacts with labor market demands, driven by changes in organizations' human capital needs.

My presentations so far include (a) AoM Boston 2019 and (b) Wharton Corporate Strategy and Innovation Conference
